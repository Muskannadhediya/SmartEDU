Our history

SmartEDU was established on 24 March 2005 through The Amity University Uttar Pradesh Act, 2005 (Act 11 of 2005) of the UP Vidhan Sabha by the Ritnand Balved Education Foundation. It was India's first private university to implement reservations and merit-based scholarships for students.
